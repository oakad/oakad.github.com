Alex Dubov's assorted thoughts and articles

In this collection of articles, I'll try to develop several ideas, which,
to my understanding, may be useful in developing better, more conveninent
computer operation environments. While I fully support the notion, that good
code is better way to express advanced ideas in computing, sometimes the
goals are so elusive, that verbal evaluation is absolutely needed, to form
a good sense of priorities and features required.
